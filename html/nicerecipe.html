Classic Chicken Parmigiana Recipe

Welcome to our delicious chicken parmigiana recipe! This traditional Italian dish is a crowd-pleaser and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups of marinara sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Dip each chicken breast into the beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs mixed with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Fry the chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce. Arrange the fried chicken breasts on top.
  6. Spoon additional marinara sauce over the chicken breasts, then sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite side dish and enjoy your delicious chicken parmigiana!
