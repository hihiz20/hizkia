Classic Grilled Cheese Recipe

Welcome to our delicious grilled cheese recipe! This timeless comfort food is quick and easy to make, perfect for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (preferably sourdough)
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar or American work well)
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Spread butter evenly on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, on the skillet.
  4. Layer cheese slices on top of the bread.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and crispy, and the cheese starts to melt, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Flip the sandwich using a spatula and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is fully melted, another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Remove from the skillet and let it cool for a minute before serving.
  9. Cut the sandwich diagonally if desired, and enjoy!
